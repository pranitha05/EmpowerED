Chess

Description: Chess is a two-player strategy board game played on an 8x8 grid. The objective is to checkmate the opponent's king.

How to Play: Each player controls 16 pieces: one king, one queen, two rooks, two knights, two bishops, and eight pawns. Move pieces according to their unique movements and aim to capture the opponent's king.

Simon Says

Description: Simon Says is a memory game where players must repeat a sequence of colors and sounds generated by the game.

How to Play: Follow the sequence of colors and sounds shown by the game. Tap the colors in the same order as they were presented to you. The sequence will get longer as you progress.

Speed Math

Description: Speed Math is a fast-paced math game where players solve simple arithmetic problems under a time limit.

How to Play: Solve as many math problems as you can within the given time. Each correct answer earns you points. Aim to beat your high score!

Array Game

Description: The Array Game involves organizing or manipulating arrays based on given instructions.

How to Play: Follow the instructions to arrange, sort, or manipulate the array elements. Complete the tasks to progress to the next level.

Remember

Description: Remember is a memory game where players have to recall and match pairs of items from a grid.

How to Play: Flip over tiles to reveal pairs of matching items. Remember their positions to match all pairs before time runs out.

Letter Train

Description: Letter Train is a word formation game where players create words by connecting letters.

How to Play: Drag and connect letters to form valid words. Complete word challenges to earn points and advance through levels.

Number Search

Description: Number Search is a puzzle game where players find hidden numbers in a grid.

How to Play: Locate and click on hidden numbers in the grid. Find all numbers within the time limit to complete the level.

Classic Pac-Man

Description: Classic Pac-Man is an arcade game where players navigate a maze to eat pellets while avoiding ghosts.

How to Play: Use the arrow keys to move Pac-Man through the maze. Eat all the pellets while avoiding the ghosts. Collect power pellets to temporarily turn the tables on the ghosts.
